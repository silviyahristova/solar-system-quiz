How to play

1. Answer all 10 questions to complete the quiz.

2. You earn 1 point for every correct answer.

3. If you want to activate or deactivate sounds during the game, use the Sound ON/OFF button at the top of the screen.

4. Keep an eye on the timer! If time runs out, you will automatically move to the next question.

5. Your score will be displayed at the end of the quiz.

6. Click "Start Again" to start a new game.

Good Luck!
